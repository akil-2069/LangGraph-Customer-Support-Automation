FAQ DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the
reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires after 30 minutes.

Q: Can I change my subscription plan at any time?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Account Settings >
Billing. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Q: What happens if my payment fails?
A: We will retry the payment 3 times over 7 days. If it continues to fail, your account will be
temporarily restricted until payment is resolved. You will receive email notifications at each
retry attempt.

Q: How do I invite team members?
A: Go to Account Settings > Team Members > Invite, enter their email address, and select
their role (Admin, Editor, Viewer). They will receive an email invitation to join.

Q: Is my data backed up?
A: Yes, all customer data is backed up daily and retained for 30 days. Enterprise customers
can request custom backup schedules.

Q: How do I export my data?
A: Navigate to Settings > Data Export, select the data range and format (CSV or JSON),
and click Export. Large exports may take up to 24 hours to generate and will be emailed
as a download link.

Q: Do you offer a mobile app?
A: Yes, our mobile apps are available for iOS and Android with core feature parity to the
web application, excluding advanced reporting and custom integrations.

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: Full refunds are available within 14 days of purchase. See the Company Policy
Document for full details. All refund requests require human supervisor approval.

Q: How do I deactivate my account temporarily?
A: Go to Account Settings > Account Status > Deactivate. This pauses billing without
deleting your data. You can reactivate at any time by logging back in.

Q: Who do I contact for urgent issues?
A: For urgent technical issues, use the in-app chat for fastest response. For billing
emergencies or escalations, requests are automatically routed to a human supervisor.
