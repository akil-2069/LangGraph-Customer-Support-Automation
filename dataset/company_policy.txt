COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

REFUND POLICY
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase if they are not satisfied
with the product. Refunds for annual subscriptions are prorated based on unused months
after the 14-day window. All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human
supervisor before processing. Refund requests typically take 5-7 business days to process
once approved.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time from their account settings. Cancellation
takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for
cancellations unless they fall within the 14-day refund window. All cancellation requests
require human supervisor approval before being finalized.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
Account closure requests result in permanent deletion of customer data after a 30-day grace
period. During the grace period, customers can reactivate their account. Account closure
requires human supervisor approval due to the irreversible nature of data deletion after the
grace period.

COMPENSATION POLICY
Service credits or compensation may be issued for verified service outages or critical bugs
that caused business impact. All compensation requests must be reviewed and approved by
a human supervisor. Compensation is typically issued as account credit, not cash refund.

ESCALATION POLICY
Requests involving legal threats, repeated unresolved issues, or requests to speak with
management must be escalated to a human supervisor immediately. These requests cannot
be resolved by automated agents.

DATA PRIVACY POLICY
Customer data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without explicit
consent. Customers may request a copy of their data or request deletion at any time in
compliance with data protection regulations.
